# Visual Style Cheat Sheet (for the design pass)

**Colors (mix freely):**
- Red / coral (#d94f4f) for hype headers
- Orange (#e87d2f) for warm callouts
- Green / sage (#9fb798 or brighter) for sections
- Navy (#28587b) for sign-offs and names
- Gold / yellow highlight (#fff3b0) for key word backgrounds
- Bronze tones for the bronze slide, silver for silver, gold for gold

**Type:**
- Mix at least 2 fonts per slide
- Wildly varied sizes within a single slide (the chaos rule)
- Hand-drawn / sharpie effect for emphasis
- All caps for award titles, lowercase for casual captions

**Stickers and decoration:**
- Doodled stars, hearts, arrows, fire emojis
- Yellow highlight strips behind key words
- Polaroid-style photo frames
- Sharpie scribbles in the margins
- Spray paint or grunge textures on section dividers

**Photos:**
- Real photos of winners wherever possible
- Selfies, candids, ugly photos welcome (chaotic vibe)
- Group photos as backgrounds for big slides

**What to avoid:**
- Em dashes (use commas, parentheses, "and", periods)
- Clean corporate slide templates
- Single-color slides without contrast
- Bullet point lists that look like a meeting deck
- Anything that looks AI-generated or stock-template
