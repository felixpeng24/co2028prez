# "Don't Die Week" — End of Sophomore Year Event

## Context
Class of 2028 end-of-year celebration doubling as a prize ceremony for contest winners from throughout the year. Casual, fun vibe — sundae bar for everyone, boba for winners. Hosted by co-presidents Felix Peng & Ashlyn Green.

---

## Event Details

- **Name:** Don't Die Week
- **Date:** Friday, May 1 (last day of classes)
- **Time:** 6:00 PM
- **Location:** Riggs Room (needs to be booked)
- **Expected turnout:** 20–30 people
- **BYOB:** Bring Your Own Bowl

---

## What's Happening

### 1. Sundae Bar (for everyone)
People show up, grab their bowl, build a sundae. Simple.

### 2. Boba Prizes (for winners)
Individual boba drinks from **Sunright** (~$7 each) for contest winners:

| Contest | Winners | Count |
|---|---|---|
| Hoodie design submissions | Logan Mansfield, Heidi R, Ellen Y | 3 |
| Vent cleaning contest | Octavia | 1 |
| Stompers — top 3 points | TBD | 3 |
| Stompers — most steps (Cross Country) | Arnav | 1 |

**Total boba recipients: 8 people**

*(May add a few more for fun random stat awards — budget can handle up to ~11)*

### 3. Stompers Awards Ceremony
- Announce top 3 winners + Arnav's Cross Country award
- **Award for #1 Stompers winner** — two options:
  - **Option A: Custom trophy** (~$10–15 from Amazon, small engraved trophy with free personalization)
  - **Option B: Printed certificate + frame** (~$5–10, cheaper and can design/print yourself)
- Fun random stats shoutouts (most steps in one day, most consistent walker, etc.)
- Spin the wheel for bonus prizes/fun

### 4. Hoodie Rerun Teaser
Mention at the event that a hoodie rerun is coming. Don't take orders yet — handle that separately after.

## Possible Ideas / Add-ons (Not Committed Yet)

Bucket of optional things we could layer on top of the core event. None of these are locked in — just brainstorming.

### Photo Strips
- Rent or borrow a photo booth setup (HMC clubs sometimes have one, or check with ASHMC)
- DIY version: phone + tripod + ring light + a laptop running a photo booth app (e.g., [Photobooth](https://photoboothonline.co/) or free Mac Photo Booth)
- Print on-site with a small portable printer (Canon Selphy, HP Sprocket — ~$100–150 or borrow one)
- Could double as a nostalgia thing — "your sophomore year survival photo"
- Backdrop: a cheap fabric/streamer wall, or just a clean corner of Riggs

### Poster Board / Memory Wall
- Big poster board (or butcher paper roll taped to the wall) where people write:
  - Best memory from sophomore year
  - Worst class they survived
  - One word for sophomore year
  - Shoutouts to classmates
- Markers/sharpies scattered around
- Take a photo of the finished board at the end for the class archive
- Low cost: ~$10–15 for poster board + markers

### Combined Vibe
- If we do both, the photo strip + poster board corner becomes the "memory station"
- People drop by between sundae and awards, leaves a nice artifact from the year
- Keep it casual — no forced participation
